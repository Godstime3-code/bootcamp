What is the bootcamp and how does it work?

The Godstime's Front-end Masters Bootcamp is a complete crash course,and everything you need to get up and running with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. By completing the course work contained in the bootcamp, along with practice, you will be well on your way into world of web development!

All of our course are taught by industry leading experts, people actively shaping the craft of web development
